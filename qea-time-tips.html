4 Micro-Moves That Actually Work
☕

15-Min Power Block

One focused template section while your chai brews. That's enough to make real progress.

🎧

Commute = Think Time

Voice-note your thoughts into the group. Raw is fine — we'll shape it together.

🤝

School Pickup Buddy Call

10 mins in the car park with your buddy. Quick check-in, real accountability.

⚖️

Weekend Partner Pact

Negotiate one 2-3 hour Saturday block with your partner. You build, they parent. Next weekend, swap. No guilt.
